## 📱 How Customers Use It

### Step 1: Get the Link
When customer signs up, share their card link:
```
https://yourapp.com/card/abc123-customer-id
```

### Step 2: Open the Link
Customer taps the link on their phone

### Step 3: Add to Home Screen (Optional)
**iPhone:**
1. Tap Share button (box with arrow)
2. Scroll down → "Add to Home Screen"
3. Tap "Add"
4. Done! Icon appears on home screen

**Android:**
1. Tap menu (3 dots)
2. "Add to Home screen"
3. Tap "Add"
4. Done! Icon appears on home screen

### Step 4: Use Anytime!
- Tap icon to open card

- Check points & rewards
- No login required!
