```

```

### 2. **You Collect Their Info**
- Business name: "Coffee Paradise"
- Logo: Upload their logo
- Brand color: "#8B4513" (brown)
- Phone: +968 9111 1111
- Email: contact@coffeeparadise.com
- Address: Muscat, Oman

### 3. **YOU Create Business in Platform**
**Your Steps:**
1. Login as Super Admin
2. Go to "Businesses" page
3. Click "Add Business"
4. Fill in their info:
   - Name, logo, brand color
   - Contact details
   - Create business slug (e.g., "coffee-paradise")
5. Set up their loyalty programs
6. Add any staff accounts they need

### 4. **YOU Generate QR Code for Them**
**Your Steps:**
1. Go to "QR Generator" in Super Admin panel
2. Select "Coffee Paradise" from dropdown
3. See custom QR code with their brand color
4. Download high-quality PNG
5. Print beautiful poster

### 5. **YOU Give Them the QR Code**
**Options:**
- Email the QR image
- Send via WhatsApp
- Print and deliver physically
- Share the signup link

**What you give them:**
- ✅ Printed QR poster
- ✅ Signup link
- ✅ Simple instructions
- ✅ Login credentials (if they want access)

### 6. **Business Displays QR at Shop**
Coffee shop owner:
- Displays QR at counter
- Puts on tables
- Shows to customers

### 7. **Customers Scan and Signup**
Customer flow:
```
See QR → Scan → Opens signup form →
Enters phone + name + birthday →
Gets digital loyalty card instantly! →
Card shows visual stamps ◉ ◉ ○ ○ ○
```

### 8. **Customer Returns to Shop**
Customer:
- Shows phone number OR
- Shows QR code from their card

Staff:
- Looks up by phone
- Adds visit/stamps
- Customer's card updates!
